After downloading the Android (WAID) What Am I Doing ? application enter an email address, a password, firstname, lastname and you can start sharing moments.

Description of Mobile App Actions:

  •   To start and stop sharing a live stream.
  •   To start and stop the camera
  • Send Location  To allow viewers of your stream to see where you are on a map
  • Whoes Accepted  Fetches information about those you have invited to view your live stream
  • Views Area  Total count of people watching the live stream
  •   To start chat conversations with viewers of the current stream
  •   To log out from the program

Mobile App Usage:

  • Click on to Start Sharing and to Start Camera
  • When you see this on the status bar and
    all the buttons are enabled you are ready to invite people to view your stream.
  • You can send an email, post on facebook or tweet on twitter using the icons on the menu bar
  • Starting and Stopping the camera has no impact on the stream
  • Once you click on this the stream is no longer active
  • If you start sharing again you will need to re-invite your viewers so that they can view your new live stream.
  • After clicking on "Chat" you should see on the status bar.
